About Bank Of SUTD


Being purpose-driven is part of our DNA. From the time of BOS' founding as the Development Bank of Singapore Universities, we have believed in the importance of good citizenship. Building on this illustrious heritage, BOS has continued to reinvent ourselves over the years with a view to creating a more sustainable bank.

People are at the heart of everything we do at BOS, which is why we dedicate ourselves to understanding what you need, and more. Whether it’s helping you with your investments or turning your business ideas into reality, we are committed to be the bank that you can trust.
